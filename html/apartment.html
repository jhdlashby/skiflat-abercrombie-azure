Skiflat Abercrombie

Apartment

The fully furnished 110 square metre chalet apartment (incl. cellar) consists of three bedrooms, two bathrooms, a combined kitchen, dining and living room all of which have individually controlled under-floor heating. In addition there is a ski cellar. It is located on the top floor of the house, which means that the front door is on the same level as the covered car parking area. This means that luggage and shopping handling is very easy.

Parking

A covered parking space is provided for the apartment right in front of the entrance.

Hallway

As you enter the apartment, there is plenty of storage space for all outside clothes and shoes. We kindly ask all of our guests to remove their outside shoes in this area in order to protect the parquet flooring. All ski boots and equipment must be left in the cellar.

Combined kitchen, dining and living room

The main room of the apartment is a large 40 square metre combined living room, dining-room and kitchen. The room has a beautiful pinewood vaulted ceiling, parquet floor and large French windows leading onto a wooden balcony. The views from this room are breathtaking with the Dachstein mountain range in the distance and the Flachau valley and town just below.
The living room has a corner sofa in which guests can relax and watch television on the wall mounted flat screen TV. A DVD player and DVD library are also provided, including a full range of kiddy friendly films. The local Cable television channels show panorama camera coverage of the local ski resorts as well as weather forecasts. This is great information when deciding where to ski.
The kitchen has been designed by an ex-chef and has everything and more (kettle, coffee-maker, knives, pots, pans, ladles etc.). The fridge, cooking hob, oven, microwave, dishwasher and extraction fan are all brand new and of good quality (Bosch). All of the work surfaces are granite, there is a huge amount of storage space and the double sink really helps with food preparation.
For dining, there is a two seater breakfast bar and a dining table that seats 8. Also provided is a children's high chair for younger guests.

Three bedrooms

All of the bedrooms have wooden floors, individually controlled under-floor heating and very good quality mattresses. All bedding materials and pillows etc. are feather free in order to cater to guests who suffer from allergies.
The Master Bedroom has a large built in wardrobe for storage, a king-size bed and the 3 metre French windows and balcony provide a great view over the valley and surrounding mountains.
The Second Bedroom is equipped with two single beds, a large built in wardrobe and cupboard.
The Third Bedroom sleeps four in two bunk beds. This room also has a built in wardrobe and chest of drawers. As per Austrian tourist regulations, the upper bunk beds are not to be used by children under six years of age.

Two bathrooms

The apartment has two bathrooms: one with a combined bath and shower, basin and W.C., the other with a walk in shower, basin and W.C. Both bathrooms have large heated towel racks.

Storage room

As you enter the hallway there is a small storage room, complete with cleaning materials, vacuum cleaner etc...

Cellar

The apartment comes with a large ski cellar. This is equipped with ski racks, ski boot dryer, iron and ironing board, washing machine and dryer.

Location

The location of the apartment is fantastic. The Piste is only 100 metres away, so there is no need to worry about driving, parking and walking with heavy equipment such as skis, snowboards, poles etc. You can literally put your skis on at the front door, ski all day and take them off again at the front door when you have finished. Once you are on the ski slope there is a short descent of 250 meters to the Valley Station of the "Achter Jet" ski lift which will take you high up the mountain.
