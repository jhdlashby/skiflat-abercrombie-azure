Attractions

There are some fantastic local attractions to visit. Just a hand full are listed below that we thought were well worth seeing, all of which are within a one hour drive of the apartment.

Lichtenstein Klamm - 20 mins

Zell am see - 45 mins

Grossglockner alpen strasse - 45 mins

Salzburg - 45 mins

Burg Hohenwerfen castle - 15 mins

Ice caves at Werfen - 15mins

Schloss Hellbrunn - 45 mins

Mauterndorf - 30 mins

Radstadt Golf course - 15 mins

Mountain Bike Park Wagrain - 15 mins

Wagraini's Graftenberg - 15 mins

Berchtesgarden - 50 mins

Therme Amade - 10 mins

The Geisterberg

The Jägersee

Dining

There are dozens of different restaurants, pubs and bars in and around Flachau. Sadly we have not yet had the chance to visit them all. Why not drop us an email after your visit and give us some feedback about your favourites?
Our favourites so far have been:

Jagdhof

Great Pizza's, very children friendly with in door and out door play areas and a great 1 Euro internet service that helps you keep up to date with your emails.

Waldgasthof

This is on the Griessenkarweg service road and at the middle chair lift station on the Griessenkar Mountain. We really liked Fr. Huber who treated us with great kindness and great food. She has a lovely indoor and outside play area with stunning views over the valley towards Sattelbauer.

Sattelbauer

This is on the opposite side of the valley and is a very friendly mountain restaurant with great hiking from either Flachau or Altenmarkt.

Winterbauer

This is a super 4 star Hotel on the top of a hill just outside Flachau. Great organics food and super views over two valleys.

Musistadl

The nearest bar to the apartment is only five minutes away and has live traditional music every night and a super playground.

Hoagascht

Recommended for the evenings by one and all.

Amenities

Supermarkets

The nearest supermarket is the SPAR which has very good quality food and drink as well as all sorts of useful things that you didn't know you needed. There are three other supermarkets in town, so there is a lot of choice.
Please note that all of the supermarkets close on Saturday at around 5.00 pm and do not open again till Monday morning. Also be careful not to get caught out as credit cards are not generally accepted in supermarkets. Maestro works, or cash!
The SPAR supermarket is now open every Sunday afternoon during the winter between 14.00 and 18.00.

Sports Shops

There is an alpine sports shop in the town, opposite the SPAR which has quite a wide selection of outdoor shoes and clothing. There is much more choice in Altenmarkt which is only a ten minute drive away.

Banks and Post Office

There is a Raifeissen Bank with an international ATM, a post office and a very good information office where visitors can find out what is going on in town. You will find this in the town centre.

The Bakery

If you prefer your breads really fresh, the bakery (just opposite the post office) is open every day, but more importantly it is also open on Sunday mornings between 7.00 and 10.30. We found this to be very useful as all of the supermarkets are closed on Sunday.

Doctors

Flachau does have a doctor, and his contact details as well as the medical centre details are in the apartment.
An alternative is to go to the Medical Centre in Altenmarkt. This is an excellent medical facility with a wide range of specialist doctors including Paedeatric. The web site is www.agzmed.at and the telephone number is +43 6452 51517700.

More Shopping

For more extensive shopping Alten Markt has a wider offering.
St. Johan has the biggest shopping in the area (other than Salzburg) and you will find pretty much everything that you might need there.
For more upmarket and traditional shopping the high street in Werfen is well worth a visit.

Children

Babies

For babies, the apartment is equipped with a portable baby cot complete with bed clothes, high chair and baby toys.

Toys, Games and Entertainment

The apartment has a huge toy box full of fun stuff for the kids to play with as well as lots of more grown up traditional games from chess to scrabble and monopoly. There is also a large flat screen TV and DVD player with a library full of family rated, PG and action films, including all of the Disney classics.

Books and Maps

Easy reading books are also provided and guests are encouraged to add to the collection of holiday books if they wish. The apartment is also well stocked with pamphlets, maps and flyers for the various events and activities that are taking place within the region.

Kindergartens

In winter the "snow spacester" has specially designed lifts for children and nursery slopes, and for the very small, there are professionally managed kindergartens that will look after the little ones while the big ones play.
